IntusXR Privacy Policy

Introduction

At IntusXR we are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. This privacy policy sets out how we use and protect any information that you give us when you use our VR application, website, or provide your contact details to us.

If we ever ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information (such as an email address), it will only be used in accordance with this privacy policy.

What User Data Do We Collect About You?

We do not collect any personal data about users. Our VR applications operates without storing or processing:

  • Names, emails, or account details
  • VR device identifiers (Meta/Oculus IDs)
  • Usage data, biometric data, or location data

Exception: If integrated services (like Meta SDKs) collect anonymized analytics, we ensure no personal data is shared.

For What Purposes Are We Processing That Data?

We do not collect or process user data.

How Can Users Request to Delete That Data?
